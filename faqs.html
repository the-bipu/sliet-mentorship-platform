Frequently Asked Questions
Your Guide to SLIET Mentors
Find answers to common questions about how MentorHub works, connecting with mentors, and more.
1. What is Sliet Mentors?
Sliet Mentors is a platform designed to connect students with experienced mentors from SLIET (Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology) to support their academic and career growth.
2. How do I connect with a mentor?
You can explore our mentor profiles based on your field of interest and send a request to connect through the Sliet Mentors platform.
3. Who are the mentors available on Sliet Mentors?
Our mentors are professionals and faculty members from various disciplines at SLIET, offering valuable insights and guidance to students.
4. Is there a fee for using Sliet Mentors?
No, Sliet Mentors is a free platform for students and mentors to connect and collaborate.
5. How do I become a mentor on Sliet Mentors?
If you are a faculty member or professional affiliated with SLIET, you can apply to become a mentor through our registration process on the platform.
